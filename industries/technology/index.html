Our Practice

Technology Advisory as a Growth Catalyst

At Chatura Indonesia, our Technology & Digital practice is built on the understanding that technology companies face unique financial, regulatory, and strategic challenges that require specialized advisory expertise. From early-stage ventures seeking seed funding to USD 130 million+ scale-ups preparing for cross-border IPO, we provide the strategic guidance that bridges ambition and execution.

Our team works closely with founders, CEOs, CFOs, and boards of technology companies across Indonesia's thriving digital economy — spanning SaaS platforms, fintech, e-commerce enablers, health-tech, ed-tech, and AI/ML ventures. We understand the metrics that matter to technology investors and the governance standards required for public market readiness.

Whether you are a fintech startup navigating Bank Indonesia licensing, a SaaS company preparing for Series B/C funding, or a digital platform eyeing a cross-border IPO on SGX or IDX, Chatura delivers the precision, transparency, and strategic depth your technology organization demands.

The Challenge

Common Technology Challenges We Address

IPO Readiness & Public Market Preparation

Scaling from startup to publicly listed company requires rigorous financial reporting, corporate governance upgrades, and compliance frameworks that satisfy exchange requirements and investor scrutiny.

Cross-Border Expansion Complexity

Multi-jurisdictional IPO, cross-border M&A, and regional market entry demand structured financial planning, tax optimization, and regulatory navigation across Southeast Asian markets.

Funding & Capital Structure

Navigating Series funding rounds, convertible notes, SAFE agreements, and optimizing capital structure for growth while managing dilution and investor expectations requires specialized expertise.

Regulatory & Compliance Burden

Bank Indonesia fintech licensing, OJK digital financial regulations, data sovereignty requirements, and PDP Law compliance create complex regulatory landscapes for technology companies.

Talent Acquisition & Retention

Competing for elite engineering, product, and data science talent requires competitive compensation structures, ESOP design, and employer value propositions that resonate with top-tier professionals.

Profitability & Unit Economics

Transitioning from growth-at-all-costs to sustainable profitability requires restructuring unit economics, optimizing SaaS metrics, and demonstrating clear paths to cash flow breakeven.

Our Solution

Integrated Technology Advisory Practice

Our integrated technology advisory practice combines deep capital markets expertise with startup-specific financial acumen. We understand the unique metrics, timelines, and stakeholder dynamics that define technology company growth — from seed stage to public listing.

From IPO readiness assessment and cross-border listing advisory to M&A execution and growth financing, we provide the precision and strategic depth that technology companies need to scale successfully across Southeast Asia and beyond.
