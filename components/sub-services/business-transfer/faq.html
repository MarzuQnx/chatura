We employ multiple valuation methodologies including discounted cash flow (DCF), market multiples analysis, and precedent transaction comparisons. Our team conducts a thorough assessment of your financials, market position, growth trajectory, and intangible assets to arrive at a defensible and realistic enterprise value.

We serve businesses across manufacturing, technology, consumer goods, healthcare, energy, and professional services. Our practice covers transactions ranging from SME exits to large corporate M&A deals, working with both private companies and institutional buyers.

Timelines vary based on transaction complexity, but most engagements run between 4 to 12 months from initial engagement through closing. The discovery and valuation phase typically takes 4-6 weeks, while due diligence and negotiation span 3-6 months depending on deal structure.

Confidentiality is fundamental to our process. All parties execute non-disclosure agreements before accessing sensitive information. We use secure data rooms for document exchange and carefully manage information flow to protect your business relationships and competitive position throughout the transaction.
